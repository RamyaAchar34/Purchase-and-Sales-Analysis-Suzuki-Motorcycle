# 📊 Purchase and Sales Analysis at Suzuki Motorcycle (BGS Motors)

## 📌 Overview

This academic project analyzes the purchase and sales operations of Suzuki Motorcycle (BGS Motors, Udupi). The study focuses on customer behavior, inventory management, sales performance, and financial analysis to understand factors influencing business growth and decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Analyze purchase and sales trends
- Study customer buying behavior
- Evaluate inventory management practices
- Examine financial performance
- Identify business challenges and provide recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Areas Covered

- Purchase Analysis
- Sales Analysis
- Customer Behavior
- Inventory Management
- Financial Analysis
- Marketing Strategies
- Business Operations

---

## 📈 Key Findings

- Customer preferences significantly influence sales performance.
- Effective inventory management improves operational efficiency.
- After-sales service plays an important role in customer retention.
- Promotional activities and financing options impact purchasing decisions.
- Business analytics supports better decision-making.
